Privacy Policy for Lookly

Last updated: [Insert Date]

1. Who We Are

Lookly is a mobile application that provides AI-powered style advice based on user-uploaded photos and contextual information such as occasion, gender, age, and country. We prioritize your privacy and collect only the data essential for delivering core app functionality.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Device ID: Used solely to track whether a user has already received their first-time bonus coins (stored securely via Unity Cloud Save).
  • Image & Occasion Context: When you upload an image for style scoring, we send the photo and contextual details (occasion, gender, age, and country) to OpenAI’s API for temporary processing.
  • App Usage Data: We use Unity Analytics to collect anonymous data about how users interact with features (e.g., how many users generate a style score or view style tips).
  • Local Storage: If you choose to save your style score results, these are stored only on your device. We do not upload or back up this data to any server or cloud platform.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Prevent multiple bonus coin claims per device.
  • Provide AI-generated fashion scores and recommendations.
  • Understand feature usage and improve app experience using Unity Analytics.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not store your uploaded images permanently on any server.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not track your behavior across other apps or services.

5. AdMob and Rewarded Ads

We use Google AdMob to show rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data to deliver personalized ads. You can learn more about Google’s practices here: Google Privacy Policy.

6. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Unity Cloud Save: To store device ID and coin status securely.
  • Unity Analytics: To collect anonymous app usage insights to improve features and user experience.
  • OpenAI API: To analyze uploaded images temporarily and provide AI-powered feedback.

7. User Control and Consent

  • By using Lookly, you consent to the processing of your data as outlined in this policy.
  • You can delete the app at any time. No personal data will be retained after deletion.
  • To request deletion of your Device ID from our system, contact us at: advita.infotech9@gmail.com

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other local laws, including:

  • The right to request deletion of any data we store.
  • The right to know what data is being processed.

Contact us at advita.infotech9@gmail.com to exercise these rights.
